Why Transparency & Privacy Matter
Extensions that aren't open source and collect user data can change policies at any time, and users can't verify what they're really doing in the background. That’s why, even after returning to browser stores, you should be skeptical of blockers with a history of privacy abuse—and why SpicyBlocker’s roadmap is focused on transparency, privacy, and an open-source future.
FAQs
Does SpicyBlocker track or sell my data?
No! SpicyBlocker operates fully client-side and respects your privacy. No tracking, no ads, no data ever leaves your device.
How are NSFW sites detected so well?
We use community-powered lists, AI-based pattern checks, and constant updates to stay ahead of *all* popular and new domains.
How can I unblock a site I need for research?
You control your custom blocklist. Simply remove the domain or keyword in the SpicyBlocker settings and it's instantly unblocked.
Is SpicyBlocker totally free?
100%! No hidden fees, no premium traps. Open source forever (coming soon)!
